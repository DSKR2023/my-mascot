## Ad Review Notes

- The top page now contains visible publisher content before any monetization code.
- Auto ads are not loaded on modal-only, game-only, empty, error, or under-construction screens.
- Terms, privacy policy, sitemap, robots.txt, and ads.txt are included.
- Re-add ad units only to content-rich sections after review, and avoid placing ads in overlays, games, navigation-only screens, or pages with little content.
